Main Features

Everything you need to manage your academic journey

Register for Courses

Easily browse and register for courses with a simple, intuitive interface. Select from available courses and complete registration in just a few clicks.

Drop Courses

Need to make changes to your schedule? Drop courses effortlessly with our streamlined process, ensuring you stay in control of your academic path.

Find Your Curriculum

Access your complete curriculum at a glance. View all required courses, prerequisites, and track your academic progress throughout your program.

Automatic Timetable Generation

Automatically generate a comfortable, conflict-free timetable with dates, classrooms, teacher names, and their contact information based on your selected courses.

Teacher Information

Access complete teacher contact information, office hours, and course details for all your registered courses in one convenient location.
