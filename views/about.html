Our Story

Built by Educators, for Educators

StudentMS was born from a simple observation: educational institutions spend countless hours on administrative tasks that could be automated.

Our founder experienced this firsthand during years of working in university administration and decided to create a solution that gives time back to educators.

Today, StudentMS is used by institutions across multiple countries to manage student data efficiently and securely.
